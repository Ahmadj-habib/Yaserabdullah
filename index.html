About Me

My Journey

Born and raised in Sweden, I developed a passion for medicine at an early age. After completing my medical education, I chose to pursue my residency at Gulf Medical University in Dubai, where I'm currently in my third year specializing in Orthopedics.

My clinical focus is on diagnosing and treating musculoskeletal conditions, with a particular interest in sports medicine and joint replacement surgeries. I believe in a patient-centered approach that combines the latest medical advances with compassionate care.

Outside of my medical practice, I enjoy staying active through swimming and hiking, which helps me better understand the physical challenges my patients face. I'm also dedicated to continuing education and regularly attend international orthopedic conferences.

My Specializations

As an orthopedic specialist, I focus on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of conditions affecting the musculoskeletal system.

Joint Replacement

Specializing in knee and hip replacement surgeries, utilizing the latest minimally invasive techniques for faster recovery and better outcomes.

Sports Medicine

Treatment and rehabilitation of sports-related injuries, helping athletes return to their activities safely and effectively.

Hand & Wrist Surgery

Precise surgical interventions for conditions affecting the hand and wrist, including carpal tunnel syndrome and tendon repairs.

Spine Disorders

Diagnosis and treatment of various spine conditions, including herniated discs, spinal stenosis, and scoliosis.

Trauma Care

Emergency treatment of fractures, dislocations, and other traumatic injuries to bones, joints, and soft tissues.

Research & Innovation

Actively involved in orthopedic research, focusing on innovative treatment methods and improved surgical outcomes.
